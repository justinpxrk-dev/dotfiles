Dark Mode Palette
dark mode base24 · 24 colors bg #1e1e1e fg #92ad8d
Engineered for low-ambient (evening / coding) viewing. Foreground sage at OKLCH(0.72, 0.05, 140°) — near photopic peak (~555 nm), where cone sensitivity is maximal under monitor luminance. Accents default to the chroma floor (0.05 OKLCH) and contrast floor (5.5:1); cool slots near the 482 nm melanopsin peak are Gaussian-capped at the floor. Body foreground hits 7:1 (WCAG AAA). ANSI 'white' and 'green' slot assignments swap relative to strict Base24 — the sage foreground occupies the white slot; a pale cream occupies the green slot so green-coded markup stays distinct from default text. See Theme Philosophy.md for the full rule set.

Color rationale
base00#1e1e1e Background macOS-native dark surface, lifted off pure black to avoid OLED halation around bright glyphs.
base05#92ad8d Foreground OKLCH(0.72, 0.05, 140°) — sage near photopic peak (~555 nm), where cone sensitivity is maximal under monitor luminance. Saturation at the chroma floor (0.05); contrast at WCAG AAA (7.16:1).
base0B#c3c59c Green (cream) The ANSI 'green' slot — disambiguated from the foreground by lifting lightness (L=0.81) and rotating hue toward yellow-green (h=110°). Pale cream so green-coded markup stays distinct from default sage text.
base08#ae8a7e Red Hue warm-shifted to 40° (toward orange) to mitigate longitudinal chromatic aberration — pure red focuses behind the retina, forcing ciliary accommodation.
base0D#848fb3 Blue Pinned to chroma and contrast floors by the Gaussian penalty — sits at melanopsin's peak (~482 nm). Cap collapses to the floor; hue alone differentiates from cyan.
base0C#709897 Cyan Same Gaussian regime as blue — chroma and contrast both at the floor. Green-leaning hue (h=195°) to maintain hue separation from blue.
base0F#8d6b61 Brown Tertiary semantic slot, deliberately below the 5.5:1 accent floor (3.5:1). Reserved for de-emphasized warm markup; not used as primary syntax foreground.
